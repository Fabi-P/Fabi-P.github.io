About the event

Join us for a 36-hour hackathon where tech meets climate action. The Coders Club Sustainability Hackathon is your chance to innovate solutions that make a real-world impact. Whether you are a developer, designer, environmentalist, or data enthusiast – this is your space to collaborate, experiment, and inspire.


This year’s theme is: “Local Solutions for a Global Challenge”.

You’ll form teams to tackle environmental issues facing communities, with a focus on energy, waste, food systems, and sustainable digital practices.

Who can join?

Anyone with a passion for tech and sustainability!

  • Developers (all levels)
  • Designers & UX specialists
  • Project managers & data scientists
  • Climate activists & curious thinkers

You can come with a team or form one on the day. No idea? No problem. We will have challenge briefs to inspire you.

What to expect?

  • 💡 Keynote talks from industry experts and local innovators
  • 🤝 Team matchmaking and ideation sessions
  • 🛠️ Mentor support from developers, designers, and sustainability experts
  • 🍕 Free food, snacks, and coffee to fuel your brain
  • 🛏️ Overnight workspace and chillout areas
  • 🏆 Prizes for best projects in key categories
  • 🌱 Opportunities to pitch your idea to real-world accelerators and investors
